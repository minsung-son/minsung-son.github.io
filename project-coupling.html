Architectural Coupling

Architecture / 2020.12

Project Description

Architectural Coupling brings together two houses, the Hawthorn House in Australia and the Villa Vals in Switzerland, to create a new experimental proposal exploring extreme privacy. The collaborative project reimagines the concept of openness by situating all openings within the interior of the house. Despite the complete lack of any outward-facing openings, the residents can access natural light and ventilation through spaces such as the central courtyard and the rooftop garden. The overall proposal challenges common architectural conventions and instead offered a unique retreat where residents can enjoy privacy while maintaining a connection with the surrounding environment.
