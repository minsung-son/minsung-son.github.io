I Need Nobody

Video / 2019.12

Project Description

People are becoming increasingly alone, albeit more digitally connected than ever. It is not rare for people to not know the names of even those who live right next door, let alone share anything with them. I Need Nobody is a short stop-motion production exploring the modern lack of community spirit.
