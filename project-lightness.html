Unbearable Lightness

Architecture / 2021.02

Project Description

Unbearable Lightness is an architectural project that explores the concepts of lightness and heaviness through material experimentation and manipulation. Korean calendar paper is crumpled to varying degrees to create a number of distinct iterations that gradually convey a sense of increasing lightness. The crumpling of paper generates unconventional visual experiences, and this process is an exploration into unconventional approaches to craftsmanship and design. The interplay of light and shadow, solids and voids, as well as the patterns, colours, and typography that exist in calendar paper, contributes to a dynamic and captivating aesthetic.
