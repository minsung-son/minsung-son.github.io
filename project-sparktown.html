Sparktown: A High-End Community Stay

Architecture / 2023.01

Project Description

Sparktown is a high-end community stay that connects the traveller and the locals of the Paju Book City through spontaneous encounters and cultural programmes. The concept of the high-end community stay is defined as a space type distinguished from other types of travel accommodation, emphasising experiential interactions among travellers and locals (unlike the resort or the hotel) while making no compromises on privacy or the overall stay experience as more affordable accommodation types (such as the guesthouse) might. The project’s cultural focus is a direct response to the overwhelming cultural resources of the Paju Book City, the national cluster for publishing houses with cultural institutions and acclaimed examples of modern architecture. More than simply being a travel accommodation, Sparktown will draw in these human and content resources and become a venue powering cultural and social events around the clock—spaces such as the common library, the social lounge, the gallery, and the central plaza will support these events and connect the traveller to the cultural Paju community.
