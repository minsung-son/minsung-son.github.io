Art Festival 2019

Video / 2019.06

Project Description

In June 2019, students and staff of North London Collegiate School Jeju took part in the school's annual Arts Festival. Dubbed the "festival of stories," the festival consisted of enriching musical performances, theatre pieces, dance shows and visual art exhibitions. This video is a visual record of the five-day event, summarised into three minutes and played at the closing ceremony of the festival.
